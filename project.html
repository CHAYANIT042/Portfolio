Workshop class with Japanese friends from Tokyo University of Technology(TUT)

We got the theme "The Fun Theory"

We talk and share a lot of idea, finally we decided to choose bike parking. The problem is bike parked in no-parking of both countries.

Add something fun and interesting, after that we got "Ferris wheel bike storage"

We hope the idea can bring people to bike park in parking

How to use

Fisrt attach your chip to the front fork. Grab the card and tap on the card scanner (1 people/1 card)

Take the bike in storage

After locking, the Ferris wheel spin 1 round during bring you to have a moment of fun. It's like you are sit in the Ferris wheel and you can enjoy to see city view.

Take the bike out storage

Grab the card and tap on the card scanner,Ferris wheel bike storage find and bring your bike for you.

Door open and you can take the bike out.

We think this idea can actually happen and be practical. We hope this idea will attract people to park their bikes in legal areas.
